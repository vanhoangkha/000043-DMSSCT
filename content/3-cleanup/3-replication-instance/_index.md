#### Delete the DMS Replication instance

1. Still in the [**Database Migration Service console**](https://console.aws.amazon.com/dms/), click on **Replication instances** in the left-hand menu.
2. Select the replication instance that you created as a part of the workshop. Then, click on the **Actions** button on the right-hand side, and choose **Delete**.

![Delete](/images/3/8.png?width=90pc)

3. Confirm that you want to delete the replication instance.

![Delete](/images/3/9.png?width=90pc)
